WHY US

Know About Us

Acier group is Calicut based progressive and forward thinking fabrication company with wealth of knowledge across fabrication sectors. We have over 1000 successful projects and our expertise in this industry is indubitable.Our design range from luxury villas to skyscrapers. We take pride in the fact that we are unwilling to compromise on the quality, be it the materials used or the finesse of the fabrication to even the installation of the works. Our commitment is to achieve the considerable energy rating asked by the architects, thus helping the environment, as well as in improving the living conditions and the well being of individuals who occupy the buildings.

OUR STORY

Our Founder CEO Mr. Sunil worked as CNC machine operator in ALICO Industries in UAE before he came back to his hometown to begin his journey as a freelancer. Now Acier group is one of the leading fabricators in the state with high expertise in all types of fabrication. We use TIG welding techniques for demanding Industrial applications and ready-to-weld needs. Its a course of hardwork and wise visionary of our team that leveled us to pave milestones within a short span of time.

SERVICES

Acier Fabrication team works with architects, designers, builders, artists and other individuals to help their creative fabrication work ideas come to life. We offer architectural fabrication for many different decorative and ornamental projects.We provide a full range of fabrication services as a ‘One-Stop Shop’ and not just extrusion profiles so that it fulfills all needs of our clients. We have a full range of fabrication that uses Aluminium, Steel, Glass and uPVC.
